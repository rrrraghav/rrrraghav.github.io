About Me


I was born in New Delhi. By the time I was 13 years old, I had also lived in Tokyo for a year in 2010 and in Bangalore for a few years. When I was 13, my family moved to New Jersey, where we have been since then.

Because of my unique childhood, I have learned about and experienced vastly different cultures and perspectives. As a result, I often find myself approaching problems from multiple different angles and trying different methods. I can also understand the reasoning behind other peoples' decisions.

My Interests


I have been playing the guitar for over a decade now, and can also produce music and play the piano. I have performed in front of large and small audiences, and some of my best memories are from shows. Currently, I play guitar and am on the e-board for NU Aaroh as the Planning and Scheduling Coordinator, a music club at Northeastern. My role as PSC is to handle all logistical details such as where equipment is stored and book spaces for events and practices.

I also love exploring new places, both near and far away. Recently, I spent a year in Oakland, California as a global experience through Northeastern. During that year, I explored portions of the San Francisco Bay Area and LA. While traveling, I really enjoy taking pictures and talking to and learning from new people.

I was captain of the cross country team at my high school, and the habit of working out that was instilled in me from that has still not left me. I enjoy being active, whether that's through running, working out at the gym, playing cricket with friends, or going rock climbing.
